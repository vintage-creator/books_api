Frequently Asked Questions:


What counts as a request?

A request is counted each time you consume an API endpoint. For example, if you make a GET request to retrieve data, that counts as one request. If you make a POST request to update data, that also counts as one request.

What happens if I exceed my monthly request limit?

If you exceed your monthly request limit, your account will be automatically upgraded to the next plan to accommodate the additional requests. You will be charged the prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my monthly request limit? Can I cancel or change my plan at any time?

Yes, you can cancel or change your plan at any time. Simply log in to your account and select the plan that you want to switch to. Your new plan will take effect immediately, and you will be charged the prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for annual plans?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount for annual plans. Contact us to learn more about our annual pricing options.
